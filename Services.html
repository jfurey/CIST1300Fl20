Services

Each experience begins with a thorough consultation, followed by a tailored experience.

Our salon is departmentalized, meaning we have color specialists and style specialists.
When booking online, if you wish to receive both color and cut, please book accordingly.

Note: Hair consultations are always part of our color services and are available complimentary prior to all first-time services.

Our color services do not include a blow dry. We have fully-stocked styling stations for you to use to blow dry your own hair.

If you would like a full professional blow dry following your color service, please request this at the time of booking.
An additional charge will be added for this service. *This is not available currently due to COVID-19*

Haircuts

All haircuts are scheduled in 45-60 minute blocks of time. Please note that some haircuts require additional time, such as thicker/longer hair or big changes.

If you'd like to have your hair styled with hot tools such as a flat iron or curling iron, please request this at the time of booking and we will accommodate.



Styling

Up-Do

$65 and Up

Each experience begins with a thorough consultation followed by a customized up-style of your choice.

Bridal

$75 and Up

Each experience begins with a thorough consultation followed by a customized bridal up-style of your choice.
Service includes adding flowers, veil, or ornaments as desired.

Note: Up-dos do not include a blow dry. Please do not wash your hair on the day of the service in order to allow for maximum friction and hold.

We strongly recommend scheduling a trial appointment in advance of your wedding for a thorough consultation and run-through of the look.
