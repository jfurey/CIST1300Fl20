Be A Model

Lure Salon aims to provide the highest skilled and experienced hairstylists in the business.
All stylists/colorists complete a rigorous training program and are required to work on models. Model nights are held Tuesdays, Wednesdays, and Fridays. Services are complimentary; however, models must be open to change - no small touch-ups or trims.
